About Me

Hi, I'm Kristofer!

I am a Game Programmer, but also consider myself as a technical game designer, from Sweden. I grew a passion for video games back when my dad introduced me to videogames. This early love for video-games led me to experiment with many different designs and core mechanics on paper until I decided to take the leap to pursue a career in the games and started to study at Futuregames in 2020.

If I would describe myself, it would be I am a problem-solver who always seeks the opportunity to improve myself and thriving to get knowledge both from coding and design.
