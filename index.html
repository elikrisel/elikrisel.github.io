About Me

Hi, I'm Kristofer!

I am a Game Programmer and self-proclaimed technical game designer from Sweden. My passion for video games began early when my father introduced me to gaming with Super Mario 3D All-Stars. Since then, I have been captivated by the many different core mechanics, stunning art direction and immersive world building that makes games memorable. This fascination inspired me to explore and experiment with game design concepts, eventually leading me to pursue a career in video games through my studies at Futuregames in 2020.

I would describe myself as a creative problem-solver, driven by a huge commitment to innovation in gameplay. My approach to game development is a balance between technical precision with imaginative design with a great player experience in mind. I believe the smallest details can make the biggest impact and I constantly strive to create mechanics and systems that foster memorable interactions. I am also dedicated to expanding my skillset to bridge the gap between coding and design to craft experiences that leave a lasting impression on players, inspiring to share the same love for video games that I shaped through my life.

Outside of game development, I enjoy watching other forms of creative media and taking hikes to experience vibrant colorful environments in the nature. These activities provide me with the opportunity to seek inspiration for various ideas, whether it is in aesthetics or sound.

Below, you will find my technical experiences and projects from my education. I am actively seeking opportunities to take my first major step in the game industry, bringing my skills and creative vision to new exciting projects!
