What is Leklådan?

Leklådan is a 1 vs 3 party game for family.

What do you do in Leklådan?

  • One player plays as a bucket and the goal for the other players is to pick up the balls and throw into the bucket to score points.

  • The bucket player needs to shoot back the balls towards the players or run them over to score points.

What was my role during the production?

  • I wrote the code for Leklådan with my co-programmer. Among other things I implemented the throwing and shooting mechanics for the players and glow shader for the items.
  • I was also in charge of the repository.

  • I participated in game design meetings and explained where technical difficulties arose.

Team Members

  • Designers: Olle Ferm, Beatrice Vester, Wes Ekman and Erik Husman

  • Programmers: Kristofer Sell and Carl Persson

  • Project Managers: Pontus Söderlind and Dom Khomsan

  • Game Project - Spring 2022
  • Project duration: 6 weeks

In Conclusion

    Our task involved with making a party game for family in all ages in six weeks period of time. I could co-operate with my team more than well and there were no crunch involved and we finished bugfixing way before the deadline.
