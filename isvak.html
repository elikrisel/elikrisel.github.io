What is Isvak?

Isvak is a first person survival game set in the post-apocalyptic ice age for hardcore players.

What do you do in Isvak?

  • The player is running around and swimming in the world to collect items to upgrade their equipment.
  • The player is also looking for other items and talk with NPC's to learn more about the world.

What was my role during the production?

  • I wrote the code for Isvak with my co-programmer. I implemented the dialogue system with NPC's and lore items.
  • I also worked on pickup mechanics for collectible and item turn-ins
  • I also worked on upgrade system to work together with the character stats and UI.
  • I participated in team meetings and explained where technical difficulties arose and was in charge with repository.
