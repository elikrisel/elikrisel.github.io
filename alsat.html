What is A Leap through Space and Time?

A Leap through Space and Time, ALSaT , is a co-operative platformer rated for everyone.

What do you do in A Leap through Space and Time?

The players are getting through magical levels through puzzles and with teleport as main mechanic to get over obstacles.

What was my role during the production?

I wrote the code for ALSaT with my co-programmer. I worked on movement for the characters and some puzzle and platformer mechanics.

I participated in design meetings and explained if technical difficulties arose.
